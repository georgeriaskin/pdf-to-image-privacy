Privacy Policy

PDF to PNG / PDF to JPG — PDF to Image

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

None. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or file contents.

How the extension works

All PDF to image conversion happens locally in your browser using PDF.js — an open-source library by Mozilla. Your PDF files are never uploaded to any server. Converted images are saved directly to your device.

Local storage

The extension uses chrome.storage.local to store anonymous usage counters (such as total number of conversions and format preference) and a flag for the review prompt. This data never leaves your device and contains no personal information.

Third-party services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No external network requests are made.

Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

storage — to save anonymous usage counters locally on your device.

downloads — to save converted image files and ZIP archives to your device.
